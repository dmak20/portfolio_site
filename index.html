About

Welcome to my upcoming portfolio site! I’m in the process of revamping things. In the meantime read a little about my past. Drop me a line if you have any questions. Thanks for visiting!

I’ve been a multimedia developer for over a decade and a half working on everything from websites to kiosks to video games. I am fluent in many different programming and scripting languages as well as graphics programs.

As of August 2013, I have completed my B.S. in Game Programming and I have found full-time employment.

Prior to taking a sabbatical to complete my degree I worked with the team at EA Mythic on Warhammer Online: Age of Reckoning and Ultima Forever: Quest for the Avatar. Working with the team at EA Mythic was one of the best experiences of my professional career. It helped me develop a real understanding of the video game industry as well as deepen my lifelong passion for video games.

My last two years were spent attending the University of Advancing Technology full-time. The experience provided by UAT was extremely humbling, challenging, and a greatly fulfilling. During my education I was exposed to many facets of the industry including game development, design, creative writing, legal issues and the myriad programming languages and API’s commonly used.

All this has happened while simultaneously studying xCode, Android, modern web development and beginning the adventure of fatherhood!

Please browse my portfolio for examples of my previous work experience and contact me if you think I may make a valuable addition to your team!
